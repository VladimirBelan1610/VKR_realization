Common LaTeX Commands and Examples

Basic Text Formatting

  • \textbf{bold text} - Bold text
  • \textit{italic text} - Italic text
  • \underline{underlined text} - Underlined text
  • \textsc{small caps} - Small caps text
  • \textcolor{red}{colored text} - Colored text

Mathematical Expressions

  • $x^2 + y^2 = z^2$ - Inline math
  • \begin{equation} \int_{0}^{\infty} e^{-x^2} dx \end{equation}
  • $\frac{numerator}{denominator}$ - Fraction
  • $\sum_{i=1}^{n} i$ - Summation
  • \begin{matrix} a & b \\ c & d \end{matrix}

Document Structure

  • \section{Title} - Section heading
  • \subsection{Subtitle} - Subsection
  • \begin{itemize} \item Point \end{itemize}
  • \begin{enumerate} \item First \end{enumerate}

Tables and Figures

  • \begin{tabular}{|c|c|} \hline A & B \\ \hline \end{tabular}
  • \begin{figure} \includegraphics{image} \caption{Caption} \end{figure}
  • \begin{table}[h] \caption{Table Title} \end{table}

Special Environments

  • \begin{theorem} Statement \end{theorem}
  • \begin{proof} Proof here \end{proof}
  • \begin{quote} Quoted text \end{quote}
  • \begin{verbatim} Exact text \end{verbatim}
